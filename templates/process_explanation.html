Welcome to the Image Rating Process

This guide will walk you through the steps you'll be taking as part of this study.

  1. Questionnaire: You'll start with a brief questionnaire to gather some initial information.
  2. Training Start Screen: A screen will inform you when the training session is about to start.
  3. Training Session: You'll go through a training session to familiarize yourself with the user interface and the task.
  4. End of Training Screen: After completing the training, you'll see a screen indicating the end of the training session.
  5. Rating Start Screen: Another screen will indicate the beginning of the actual image rating process.
  6. Rating Process: You'll rate a series of images based on the criteria provided during training.
  7. Completion: Once all images have been rated, you'll see a completion screen thanking you for your participation.
