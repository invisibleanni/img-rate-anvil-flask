Welcome to the training session. Before we begin, here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • The training session is designed to familiarize you with the types of images you'll be rating.
  • You'll see examples of both real and fake images.
  • Take your time to carefully review each example, as it will help you during the actual rating process.

Rating Tips

As you evaluate each image, consider the following aspects:

  • How realistic does the image appear?
  • Are there any visible artifacts or inconsistencies?
  • How does the image compare to a real-world counterpart?
